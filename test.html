## Why does $\sum_i^ne_iX_i = 0$? Here is the derivation that illustrates why $\sum_i^ne_iX_i = 0$. \(\sum_i^n e_iX_i = \sum_i^ne_iX_i - \sum_i^ne_i\bar X_i\) because \(\sum_i^n e_i = 0\) $\sum_i^n e_iX_i = \sum_i^n e_i(X_i - \bar X)$ $\sum_i^n e_iX_i = \sum(Y_i - \bar Y)(X_i - \bar X) - \beta_1\sum_i^n(X_i - \bar X)^2$ since $\beta_0 = \bar Y - \beta_1\bar X$ $\sum_i^n e_iX_i = 0$ since $\beta_1 = \frac{\sum_i^n e_i(Y_i - \bar Y)(X_i - \bar X)}{\sum_i^n e_i(X_i - \bar X)^2}$
